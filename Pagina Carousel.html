About me Personal informations

Personal Information
I was born on December 14th 1997 in Monza, which is where I'm currently living. I've always been a creative and active person. This trade has led me towards sports and other activities such as singing, piano practice and musical theatre. I've practiced swimming since my early years, gymnastics from age 8 to age 15 and later at age 18, volleyball from age 16 to age 17. For the last two years I've practiced Crossfit.
I'm really passionate about music, cinema, acting, art and litterature, especially English litterature.
Education
After finishig secondary school I started High School at Liceo Scientifico Paolo Frisi in Monza before moving to Liceo Scientifico Federigo Enriques on my fourth year. After my graduation I took a year off to understand what I really wanted to study next. During this year I worked as much as I could and explored my passions.
In Sepember 2017 I started my journey as a Communication Designer at Politecnico di Milano.
